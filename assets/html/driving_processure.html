Issuance of Learner’s Permit ⌄
Necessity for Learner’s License A Learner’s License is issued to learn driving of Motor Vehicles. It is essential for obtaining a Driving License. Procedure • Visit District Transport Office (RTO) • Submit required documents and prescribed fees • Biometrics will be captured (Photo, Thumb impression, Signature) • Appear for Learner’s License Test (theoretical) • Test based on road signs, traffic rules & regulations • Learner’s License issued after passing test • If failed, re-test allowed after one week Age Eligibility • 16 years – Two wheeler without gear • 18 years – Two wheeler with gear, Motor Car, Non-transport vehicles • 20 years – Transport vehicles (8th standard pass + 1 year LMV experience) Other Requirements • Knowledge of traffic rules, signals and signs Where to Apply • Concerned District Transport Office (RTO) Validity • Learner’s License valid for 6 months
Issuance of Driver’s License ⌄
Procedure • Visit District Transport Office with documents • Fees submission and verification • Challan issued with date, time & venue of driving test • Bring own vehicle on test date • Test includes driving skills, traffic rules, vehicle systems & safety • Driver’s License issued after passing test Eligibility • Valid Learner’s License required • Apply after 30 days and within 180 days of LL issue • Applicant must be conversant with driving and traffic rules • License issued within 15 working days • Maximum disposal time: 30 working days Documents Required • Form No. 4 – Application Form • Form No. 1A – Medical Certificate (Commercial) • Valid Learner’s License • Two passport size photographs • Form No. 5 – Driving School Certificate (Commercial) Fee • One class of vehicle – Rs. 340/- • Two classes of vehicle – Rs. 440/- Validity • Private Vehicles – 20 years or till age of 50 • Commercial Vehicles – 3 years
Renewal of Driving License ⌄
Where to Apply • District Transport Office (RTO) • Apply between 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM • Can renew at any RTO • Address change must be updated Documents Required Non-Transport • Form 9 – Renewal Application • Form 1 – Physical Fitness Declaration • Existing Driving License • Three passport size photographs (Book form) Transport • Form 9 – Renewal Application • Form 1A – Medical Certificate • Driving License • Three passport size photographs • Driver Refresher Training Certificate (HMV)
Registration of Motor Vehicle ⌄
Documents Required • Form 20 – Registration Application • Form 21 – Sale Certificate • Insurance Certificate • Passport size photographs • Dealer Invoice / Money Receipt • Temporary Registration (if applicable) • Customs Clearance Certificate (Imported vehicles) • Bank / Finance documents (HP / Lease) • Entry tax receipt (Other state vehicles) • PAN Card copy (Vehicle cost above 4 lakhs) • Design approval (Trailer / Semi-trailer) Procedure • Vehicle produced at RTO for inspection • Physical inspection by Inspecting Authority • Registration number and receipt issued • Receipt acts as proof till RC delivery Assignment of Registration Mark • Unique Registration Mark allotted by Registering Authority as per State & Central Government norms
